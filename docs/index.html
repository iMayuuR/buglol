Installing in Cursor, Antigravity & other IDEs

Open the Extensions panel (sidebar icon or Ctrl+Shift+X), search for BugLOL or error-sound-effect, then click Install. For Open VSX–based editors (e.g. Windsurf, VSCodium), you can also install from the link above.
